â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Questions

Q: How long can I reserve a car?

A: Cars are locked for 10 minutes during booking process. Complete booking within this time.

Q: Can I modify my booking?

A: Contact support to modify confirmed bookings. Pending bookings can be cancelled and rebooked.

Q: What if I'm late for pickup?

A: Contact your assigned driver immediately. Late fees may apply after 30 minutes.

Q: Can I extend my rental?

A: Yes, contact driver or support to extend. Additional charges apply based on hourly/daily rates.

Payment & Pricing

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Cash payments not accepted.

Q: When am I charged?

A: Payment is processed when booking is confirmed. Refunds available for cancellations.

Q: Are there hidden fees?

A: No hidden fees. Total price shown includes all charges except optional extras.

Q: What about fuel costs?

A: Fuel is included in daily rates. Per-km pricing includes fuel costs.

Safety & Insurance

Q: Are cars insured?

A: Yes, all vehicles have comprehensive insurance coverage.

Q: What if there's an accident?

A: Contact emergency number immediately. Follow insurance procedures provided.

Q: Do I need a driving license?

A: Valid driving license required. International license accepted for tourists.

Q: Age restrictions?

A: Minimum age 21 years. Drivers under 25 may have additional requirements.
