🗄️ Database Schema

Customer ├── id (UUID) ├── userId (String) ├── fullName (String) ├── email (String, unique) ├── phone (String) └── password (String, hashed)
Car ├── id (UUID) ├── make, model, year ├── licensePlate (String, unique) ├── category (economy/sedan/suv/luxury) ├── dailyRate, kmRate (Decimal) ├── capacity (Int) ├── locationId (UUID) ├── features (JSON) └── isActive (Boolean)
Booking ├── id (UUID) ├── carId, customerId, driverId ├── startTime, endTime (DateTime) ├── pricingMode (daily/km) ├── estimatedKm (Int) ├── totalPrice (Decimal) └── status (PENDING/CONFIRMED/COMPLETED/CANCELLED)
Location ├── id (UUID) ├── name (String) ├── address (String) ├── lat, lng (Decimal) └── cars[] (Relation)
Driver ├── id (UUID) ├── name, phone (String) ├── licenseNumber (String, unique) ├── currentCarId (UUID) ├── active (Boolean) └── bookings[] (Relation)

📖 User Guide

🧑💼 Customer Guide

1. Getting Started

  • Register: Visit /register and create account with email, name, phone
  • Login: Use email and password at /login
  • Dashboard: Browse available cars at /dashboard

2. Booking a Car

  • Search: Use filters for location, category, dates
  • Select Car: Click "Book Now" on desired vehicle
  • Choose Dates: Set start/end date and time
  • Pricing Mode: Select daily rate or per-kilometer
  • Confirm: Review total price and confirm booking

3. Managing Bookings

  • View History: Check all bookings at /bookings
  • Cancel: Cancel pending bookings (not confirmed ones)
  • Driver Info: Get assigned driver contact details
  • Rebook: Quickly book same car again
💡 Tip: Book early for better availability and rates!

🚗 Driver Guide

1. Dashboard Overview

  • Current Booking: See active trip details at top
  • Trip Stats: View total trips, completed, earnings
  • Car Info: Check assigned vehicle details

2. Managing Trips

  • Accept Bookings: Automatically assigned by system
  • Customer Contact: Phone number provided for communication
  • Complete Trip: Mark as completed when finished
  • Earnings: 70% of booking total (30% platform fee)

3. Best Practices

  • Punctuality: Arrive on time for pickups
  • Communication: Keep customers informed
  • Vehicle Care: Maintain cleanliness and fuel
  • Safety: Follow traffic rules and safety protocols
💡 Tip: Higher ratings lead to more bookings!

👨💼 Admin Guide

1. Dashboard Monitoring

  • Real-time Stats: Monitor bookings, cars, users
  • Active Bookings: Track ongoing reservations
  • Fleet Status: Check car availability

2. Booking Management

  • View All: See complete booking history
  • Status Updates: Confirm, cancel, or complete bookings
  • Customer Support: Handle disputes and issues

3. Fleet Management

  • Car Status: Activate/deactivate vehicles
  • Driver Assignment: Manage driver-car relationships
  • Location Management: Add/edit pickup locations

4. Analytics & Reports

  • Revenue Tracking: Monitor daily/monthly income
  • Popular Cars: Identify high-demand vehicles
  • Customer Insights: Analyze booking patterns
💡 Tip: Use analytics to optimize pricing and fleet allocation!

🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

Common Issues

"Car not available"

• Car may be locked by another user (wait 10 minutes)

• Try different dates or similar cars

"Booking failed"

• Check internet connection

• Verify payment details

• Contact support if persists

"Driver not assigned"

• Wait for automatic assignment (up to 30 minutes)

• Contact admin if no driver after 1 hour

Technical Support

Login Issues

• Clear browser cache and cookies

• Try incognito/private mode

• Reset password if needed

Payment Problems

• Verify card details and expiry

• Check sufficient balance

• Try alternative payment method

App Performance

• Refresh the page

• Check internet speed

• Update browser to latest version

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Questions

Q: How long can I reserve a car?

A: Cars are locked for 10 minutes during booking process. Complete booking within this time.

Q: Can I modify my booking?

A: Contact support to modify confirmed bookings. Pending bookings can be cancelled and rebooked.

Q: What if I'm late for pickup?

A: Contact your assigned driver immediately. Late fees may apply after 30 minutes.

Q: Can I extend my rental?

A: Yes, contact driver or support to extend. Additional charges apply based on hourly/daily rates.

Payment & Pricing

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Cash payments not accepted.

Q: When am I charged?

A: Payment is processed when booking is confirmed. Refunds available for cancellations.

Q: Are there hidden fees?

A: No hidden fees. Total price shown includes all charges except optional extras.

Q: What about fuel costs?

A: Fuel is included in daily rates. Per-km pricing includes fuel costs.

Safety & Insurance

Q: Are cars insured?

A: Yes, all vehicles have comprehensive insurance coverage.

Q: What if there's an accident?

A: Contact emergency number immediately. Follow insurance procedures provided.

Q: Do I need a driving license?

A: Valid driving license required. International license accepted for tourists.

Q: Age restrictions?

A: Minimum age 21 years. Drivers under 25 may have additional requirements.

📋 Quick Reference

Booking Status Meanings

PENDING - Awaiting driver assignment
CONFIRMED - Driver assigned, ready to go
COMPLETED - Trip finished successfully
CANCELLED - Booking cancelled

Car Categories

Economy: Budget-friendly, 4 seats, basic features

Compact: Small family cars, 5 seats, good mileage

Sedan: Comfortable, 5 seats, premium features

SUV: Spacious, 7 seats, off-road capable

Luxury: Premium brands, high-end features

Van: Large groups, 10-20 seats, events

Pricing Modes

Daily Rate: Fixed price per 24-hour period

Per KM: Pay based on distance traveled

Best For Daily: Long trips, multiple days

Best For KM: Short trips, city driving
